Shipwrecked!

"While sailing in the South Pacific, your ship sank in a fierce storm. You managed to secure yourself to a piece of the mast with a rope, and floated unconscious to this island. Miraculously, you're unharmed. But you're very tired, cold, and hungry. All you have with you are the tattered clothes on your back. There appears to be a storm building to the east...you'd better act quickly and make good decisions if you intend to survive!"
